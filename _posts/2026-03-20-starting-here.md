I wanted a place on the public Internet that feels closer to a notebook than a profile page.

Most personal websites try to do too much. They become landing pages, resumes, product catalogs, and social media mirrors all at once. I wanted the opposite: a quiet place to publish things worth keeping.

So this site will stay narrow. Essays when I have something real to say. Build notes when I learn something the hard way. Short references when I find a tool, paper, or workflow that deserves to be remembered.

Simple is a feature here. Fewer moving parts means fewer reasons to stop writing.

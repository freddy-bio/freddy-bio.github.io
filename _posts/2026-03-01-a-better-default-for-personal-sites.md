title: "A Better Default for Personal Sites"
date: 2026-03-01 09:30:00 +0000
categories: notes
---

The best personal sites are usually smaller than people expect.

A short archive of writing ages better than a page full of widgets, summaries, and repeated biography. If the writing is any good, it already explains how the person thinks. That is more useful than a decorative overview.

The default should be plain text, clean links, and a structure that makes publishing easy. Most websites get worse as their owners add more machinery around the actual point.

So the standard I like is simple: can the site get out of the way and let the work speak?
